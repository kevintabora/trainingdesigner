Cognitive Task

Mental processes learners engage in to acquire, understand, and apply knowledge, such as remembering, analyzing, or creating.

Learner Activity

Specific tasks or exercises learners undertake, designed to help them meet the learning objectives by actively engaging with the material.

Delivery Method

The format or approach used to present learning content to learners.

Media

The tools or channels used to deliver content, such as videos, podcasts, or printed materials. Focuses on the physical or digital format rather than the training approach.

Type of Content

Categorizes the nature of the learning material, such as factual information, procedural instructions, or problem-solving scenarios.

Type of Content vs. Cognitive Tasks

Illustrates the relationship between content types and cognitive tasks. Helps identify emphasis and gaps for a balanced approach to learning objectives.
